Concept devolopment - flow 3

This project was about the 17 sustainable development goals and how we could implement them in an app, and thereby help solving the problems. We used Adobe XD to sketch out the prototype and a lot of techniques to determine the design. We had to:

  • Describe audience segment
  • Present idea generation process
  • Show early visualization
  • Present information architecture
  • Show testresults
  • Give a short walk through of the Adobe XD project

My part

My part in this project was overall design, though we did most of the work together. The only individual thing I did, was making the "upcoming events", as seen on the pictures, and making the "profil" page. Besides that, I made the flowchart, navigationbar, the calendar, and took part in almost everything else. Everything else than the flowchart was make in Adobe XD, which also made it easier for us because we had everything in one place.
