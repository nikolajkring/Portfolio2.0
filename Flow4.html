SoMe video production - flow 4

This project was about picking a company to promote. We made a landingpage, aswell as commercial videos (5,15, and 30sec) for the company.
We had to:

  • Describe audience segment
  • Describe of company and product
  • Present idea generation process
  • Show early visualization (sketches and wireframes)
  • Present of visual design (style, colors, design principles)
  • Do a user tests and show results
  • Present out style guide
  • Produce 3 videos 5, 15, and 30 seconds long
  • Produce a landingpage for the videoes

My part

My part of the project was to make the 5 and 15 second videos. I also described our audience, our company and product, made early visualization, and made part user test.


Editing

For the editing I used Adobde After Effects. I have a lot of experience in it, and therefore used it instead of Adobe Premiere because i knew I was going to mask a lot and 3d track.
