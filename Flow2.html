Build your portfolio - flow 2

In this assignment we had to take out portfolio prototype and make it a reality. We were assigned to use HTML and CSS to produce our websites.
We had to:

  • Produce a online version of the portfolio on GitHub
  • Have a repository for your portfolio project
  • Have an appropriate project structure file names, folder, and so on.
  • Make sure that the the code is error-free and validated

Beside that, we also had to have some things in consideration

  • Who is going to see my Portfolio
  • How do I present myself the best way
  • How do I communicate what I have learned so far and what I hope to learn in the future?
  • What visual style and mood do I want on my portfolio?
  • How can I give my portfolio a personal touch?

What I did

First of I made sure to meet all the requirements. Then I thought of who was going to see my Portfolio at that point, which was our teachers and the other student, so I decided on writing it in Danish. I wanted to present myself in a creative way, with lots of effects, as I like going the dept with whatever I'm working on. I made a onepager(portfolio), so it was quick and easy to see my work and contact me. I also tried to give it my personal touch with the orange colour (my favourite) and the many effects, which became the theme and mood of the website.
