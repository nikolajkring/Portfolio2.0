Our first assignment was to take a lot of pictures of Copenhagen. Afterwards we had to pick out a specific picture to edit in Photoshop.
We had to:

  • Remove an element
  • Add an element
  • Colour correct the image
  • Cut and rotate the image
  • Format the size to 4:3
  • Upload a before and after picture of the image in < 1 m pixel

What I did

  • Removed the "giv din PANT videre" box

  • Replaced the number 2 sign with a number 5
